Our Project

Our website is made up of 2 main sections. The Learn section allows students to learn the indigenous words from the language dataset in 3 ways. The first allows users to hover over an image of a body that will appear with the indigenous words for each body part. The second is a card matching game to match the English word to the picture with the indigenous word. The third is the quiz which gives students as well as teachers the ability to track their progress, making Dreamtime a very useful tool for the classroom.

The Language section provides interesting additional information relating to each language and gives the full wordlist available from the dataset. Teachers and parents can access this section to determine whether a certain language is suitable for what they would like to teach to their children.
